02 : About me

Passion for Linux and System Administration

I've always been drawn to how things work under the hood, and Linux quickly became my favorite system to explore. I enjoy learning about system administration tasks, digging into config files, and finding better ways to manage systems. I earned my CompTIA Linux+ certification to help grow my skills and show my dedication to the field. Most of what I’ve learned has come from hands-on practice, and I’m always looking for new ways to improve.

Learning by Doing in the Home Lab

One of the ways I’ve taught myself the most is through home labbing. I set up different services and test tools in my own environment just to see how things work and how I can break—and fix—them. It’s where I experiment, make mistakes, and learn fast. I believe this kind of hands-on learning is one of the best ways to grow.

Always Growing in Tech

I consider myself a fast learner, especially when it comes to technology. Whether it’s picking up a new tool or solving a problem I’ve never seen before, I enjoy the challenge. I’m especially excited by the world of Linux, but I also have experience with scripting, privacy tools, and some programming. I try to keep things simple, stay curious, and always look for the next thing to learn.
